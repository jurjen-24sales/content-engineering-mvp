The CTA is earned by the post, not bolted on. Map CTA type to post category.

## Awareness / TOFU Posts

**CTA type:** Reflection question
**Purpose:** Drive comments, not clicks

Examples:
- "Which of these resonates most with your team?"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Where are you seeing this play out?"

---

## Consideration / MOFU Posts

**CTA type:** Soft engagement
**Purpose:** Start conversations, surface intent

Examples:
- "If you want the full breakdown, DM me."
- "P.S. I wrote a deeper guide on this. Link in comments."
- "We're running a live session on this next week. Drop a comment if interested."

---

## Decision / BOFU Posts

**CTA type:** Direct ask
**Purpose:** Convert known intent

Examples:
- "We help {{ICP}} do exactly this. DM me if you want to see how."
- "If this sounds like your team, let's talk. Link in comments."

---

## Lead Magnet Posts

**CTA type:** Comment trigger or DM
**Purpose:** Capture leads with value exchange

Examples:
- "Drop '{{KEYWORD}}' below and I'll send it over."
- "DM me '{{keyword}}' for the template."

---

## Rules

1. Never hard-sell in the first 50% of a post
2. One CTA per post maximum
3. TOFU posts = questions only, no links
4. Lead magnet CTA only after 10+ give posts
5. Match CTA energy to post energy — a vulnerable story post doesn't end with "book a demo"
