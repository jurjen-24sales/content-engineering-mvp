## Structural Patterns

- **Listicle format** dominates: 83-90% of top 4 creators use numbered/bulleted structures
- **Cliffhanger hooks** (trailing "…") appear in 44% of all posts — default pattern
- **Comment CTA giveaways** ("comment X to get Y") inflate engagement — Michel Lieben & Alex Vacca use this to hit 2000+ comments
- **Questions in hook** (22% of Michel's posts) drive comments but are inconsistent for likes
- **Video underperforms images** for likes but drives more comments per post
- **No emojis in hooks** — consistent across all top creators

## Key Benchmarks for Content Engine

Use these as grading baselines:

- **Good post:** 150+ likes, 20+ comments
- **Strong post:** 300+ likes, 50+ comments  
- **Viral post:** 600+ likes, 100+ comments
- **Hook quality:** Must match or exceed "Absolute statement" pattern (184 avg)
- **Optimal length:** 800-1500 chars (medium)
- **Format default:** Image/carousel + listicle structure
- **Cliffhanger trailing "…"** should be default hook ending
