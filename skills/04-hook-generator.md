# Hook Generator

## Purpose

Generate 20+ hook variations for any content idea using 50 battle-tested templates. You pick the strongest opening.

The hook gives people a reason to redirect attention from whatever they're doing toward your content. Effectiveness = percentage of people who start consuming.

## When to Use

- Step 4 in the content pipeline (after ideation, before copy)
- When testing new angles on an existing idea
- When a post grader flags hook strength below 7/10

## Prerequisites

- Approved idea from the ideation session (topic + brief)
- Client's `voice-profile.md` (hooks must match their voice)
- Client's `icp.md` (hooks must speak to the right buyer)

## Data-Backed Hook Rules

From the 366-post creator analysis (7 GTM creators, April 2026 — see `creator-benchmark.md`):

| Hook Pattern | Avg Likes | Count | Top Example |
|-------------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Hidden truth / contrarian | 512 | 4 | "Europe is quietly building world-class GTM tools." |
| "The [X] of [Y]" anatomy | 371 | 6 | "The anatomy of a $7M ARR lead gen agency:" |
| Resource/Guide offer | 290 | 4 | "The 2026 GTM Flywheel Playbook" |
| Number-led | 261 | 23 | "110 leads in a single week … with zero ad spend." |
| News/Launch | 260 | 9 | "Apollo just launched their MCP server." |
| Cliffhanger (trailing …) | 237 | 160 | Default pattern — 44% of all posts use it |
| Builder/Maker | 228 | 7 | "I built Claude Skills that create a full GTM team..." |
| Death/Obituary (R.I.P.) | 194 | 18 | "R.I.P. LinkedIn experts." |
| Absolute statement | 184 | 20 | "Most B2B companies still don't understand content." |
| Question | 161 | 19 | "Can you run your entire GTM with Claude Code?" |
| Direct address (you) | 145 | 36 | "Claude Code can connect your entire GTM stack." |
| Explainer (Here's how/why) | 119 | 14 | "This is why I named my company Workflows.io." |

**Rules:**
- No emojis in hooks — consistent across all 7 top creators
- No "I'm excited to announce" (nobody cares about your excitement)
- Trailing "…" or incomplete thought = default cliffhanger pattern (44% of posts, 237 avg likes)
- Include 2+ headline components: Recency, Relevancy, Celebrity, Proximity, Conflict, Unusual, Ongoing
- Credibility proof in first 3 lines (number, result, or experience)
- Contrarian/hidden truth hooks have highest ceiling (512 avg) but lowest volume — use strategically
- Number-led hooks are the reliable workhorse (261 avg, 23 posts) — best risk/reward
- Questions drive comments but are inconsistent for likes (161 avg)
- Match the popular format of the platform

## The 50 Templates

### DESIRE (Templates 1-18)

What they want. Results they're chasing. Outcomes they dream about.

**Result-Lead Hooks:**

1. `{{specific_number}} {{outcome}} in {{timeframe}}. Here's every {{thing}} that made it possible:`
   → *"$153,000 MRR in 87 days. Here's every tactic that made it possible:"*

2. `We {{verb}} {{impressive_number}} {{thing}}. These {{count}} {{items}} got us {{result}}, consistently.`
   → *"We've sent over 23M cold emails. These 54 intent signals got us 5-11% reply rates, consistently."*

3. `{{number}} {{unit}} in a single {{timeframe}} ... with zero {{expected_cost}}.`
   → *"110 leads in a single week... with zero ad spend."*

4. `I spend ${{amount}} every single month on {{thing}} and close {{multiplier}} that in {{metric}}. Here's everything worth knowing about {{topic}}:`
   → *"I spend $18,161 every month in marketing and close 3x that in MRR."*

5. `{{count}} {{accounts/clients}} added ${{amount}} {{metric}} in {{timeframe}}:`
   → *"24 accounts added $153,000 MRR in 90 days:"*

6. `Our team {{action}} {{impressive_frequency}}. We {{result}} from it.`
   → *"Our team posts 100+ times a month on LinkedIn. We book +120 meetings from it."*

**"Here's Exactly" Hooks:**

7. `Here's a rapid-fire list of {{number}} {{things}} that will help you {{outcome}} today.`
   → *"Here's a rapid-fire list of 43 growth hacks that will help you scale revenue today."*

8. `{{count}} {{playbooks/tactics}} you can plug into {{tool}} today.`
   → *"26 go-to-market playbooks you can plug into Clay today."*

9. `If I wanted to {{goal}} from scratch, here's exactly what I'd do:`

10. `Here's the exact {{workflow/system/process}} we use to {{outcome}}:`

11. `I built the entire {{thing}} inside {{tool}}. Here's how it works:`

**Credibility-First Hooks:**

12. `{{years}} years and {{impressive_number}} {{things}} later. Here's what I've learned.`
    → *"5 years and 8 million emails a month later."*

13. `I've run {{hundreds/thousands}} of {{things}}. These {{count}} I keep coming back to:`

14. `{{timeframe}} testing {{thing_A}} vs {{thing_B}} to {{goal}}...`
    → *"300+ hours testing Claude Code vs Cursor to run our GTM..."*

15. `We manage {{thing}} across {{number}}+ clients at {{company}}.`

**Outcome-Promise Hooks:**

16. `My {{process}} that generated {{number}}+ {{outcome}} in {{year}}`

17. `The {{adjective}} thing I did for my {{business/career}} was {{unexpected_action}}.`

18. `I {{unusual_action}} for {{timeframe}}. Here's what changed.`

### CURIOSITY (Templates 19-36)

What they need to know. Gaps in their understanding. Behind-the-scenes reveals.

**Story Openers (highest engagement — 652 avg):**

19. `I was once a {{role}}, and now I {{current_state}}. {{surprising_observation}}.`

20. `Yesterday, {{impressive_number}} people watched {{person}} {{action}}. Then...`
    → *"Yesterday, 1400 people watched Kenny Damian build an entire outbound campaign."*

21. `I just {{personal_milestone}} with this exact {{system/approach}}.`
    → *"I just crossed 60,000 followers on LinkedIn with this exact tech stack."*

22. `Had a call with a {{role}} yesterday. They said something that stopped me cold.`

23. `A {{prospect/client}} told me {{surprising_thing}}. Then this happened.`

24. `Day {{number}} of {{experiment}}. Today I learned...`

**Question Hooks:**

25. `Can you {{ambitious_goal}} with {{tool}}?`
    → *"Can you run your entire GTM with Claude Code?"*

26. `How do you {{impressive_outcome}} with {{approach_A}} + {{approach_B}} running together?`

27. `What happens when you {{unexpected_combination}}?`

**Bold Claim Hooks:**

28. `The best {{thing}} in {{year}} are {{unexpected_comparison}}.`
    → *"The best GTM teams in 2026 are run like eng teams."*

29. `The best {{category_A}} is the one your competitor doesn't have.`

30. `R.I.P. {{thing_being_disrupted}}.`
    → *"R.I.P. LinkedIn experts."*

31. `{{category}} is quietly {{doing_unexpected_thing}}.`
    → *"Europe is quietly building world-class GTM tools."*

32. `Most {{audience}} still don't understand {{thing}}. The winners are {{doing_this}}.`

**Contrarian Hooks:**

33. `Everyone told me {{common_advice}}. I did the opposite. Here's what happened.`

34. `Your {{thing_they_trust}} directly affects {{unexpected_outcome}}. Not indirectly. Directly.`

35. `Here's something nobody talks about in {{niche}}.`

36. `{{common_practice}} is a death sentence. If it's not {{alternative}}, scaling will feel like pushing a boulder uphill.`

### FEAR (Templates 37-50)

What they're afraid of losing. Mistakes they're making. Things they're getting wrong.

**Mistake/Warning Hooks:**

37. `{{percentage}} out of {{large_number}} {{prospects/users}} {{negative_action}}. {{remaining}}% undetected. Here's every tactic that made that possible:`
    → *"3 out of 18,000+ prospects flagged our messages as AI-generated. 99.98% undetected."*

38. `Most {{role}} skip {{critical_step}} and wonder why {{outcome}} don't convert.`

39. `{{number}} things I stopped doing that {{positive_outcome}}:`

40. `Your {{thing}} is broken and you don't know it. Here's how to check:`

41. `I made every mistake on this list. Cost me {{amount/time}}. Save yourself the lesson.`

**"Most Teams" Hooks:**

42. `Most B2B teams run {{low_number}} {{thing}}. Maybe {{slightly_more}} if they're "advanced." We run {{impressive_number}}.`
    → *"Most B2B teams run 1 outbound play. Maybe 2 if they're advanced. We built 26."*

43. `{{high_percentage}}% of {{audience}} are {{doing_wrong_thing}}. Here's what the other {{low_percentage}}% know:`

44. `Stop {{common_action}}. Start {{better_action}}. The data is clear.`

**Loss Aversion Hooks:**

45. `Every {{time_unit}} you're not {{doing_thing}} is a {{thing_lost}}.`

46. `Your competitors are already {{doing_thing}}. Here's how far behind you are:`

47. `I {{negative_outcome}} because I {{common_mistake}}. Here's the expensive lesson.`

**Challenge Hooks:**

48. `I know you'll bookmark this and never read it again. Pick {{number}} and execute.`

49. `This will take you {{short_time}} to implement. Most people still won't do it.`

50. `Everything in this post is free. The only cost is actually doing it.`

## Generation Process

### Step 1: Classify the idea
- What emotional trigger does this idea serve? (Desire / Curiosity / Fear)
- What hook pattern fits best? (Story / Number / Result / Bold claim / Contrarian / Question)

### Step 2: Pull relevant templates
- Select 8-10 templates from the matching emotional trigger
- Pull 2-3 templates from adjacent triggers for variety

### Step 3: Generate 20+ variations
For each selected template:
- Fill with specific data from the idea brief
- Generate 2-3 variations with different angles
- Include at least one variation with specific numbers
- Include at least one story opener variation

### Step 4: Rank and select
Rank all variations against:
1. **Specificity** — does it name numbers, tools, outcomes?
2. **Voice match** — does it sound like the person?
3. **Scroll-stop power** — would a busy buyer stop for this?
4. **Promise clarity** — is it clear what the post will deliver?

### Step 5: Output top 5
Present the top 5 hooks ranked by strength. Include the template number used and the emotional trigger for each.

## Hook Anti-Patterns

- No emojis in hooks
- No "I'm excited to announce..."
- No vague thought leadership ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- No questions without a strong follow-up line
- No hooks that could be anyone's post — specificity is everything
- No hooks longer than 2 lines (the hook is the first line or two, not the first paragraph)
- No clickbait that the post can't deliver on
